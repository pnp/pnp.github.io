ABOUT PARKERSPLACE

What is ParkersPlace?

ParkersPlace is an online shop providing apparel and gear related to the PnP Community.

Where do the profits for the store go?

All profits from ParkersPlace are donated back to the Community via the PnP Rewards program and also donated to the Code.org charity

Can I submit my design to be included in the Community Design Collection?

Absolutely!

Please submit your design details using the Community Design Collection Submission Form and we will get back to you with additional details.

Community by the community support

PnP is supported in similar ways as other open source projects done by Microsoft with support from the community by the community.

Recommended transparency

There are numerous partners that utilize PnP within their solutions for customers. Support for this is provided by the Partner. When PnP material is used in deployments, we recommend being clear with your customer/deployment owner on the support model.
